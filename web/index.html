Client Assessment& Movement Screen.

Fill this out during or after the client's movement assessment. Every field below maps directly onto their personalized 15-page Body & Movement Plan.

01 Client Basics

Who are we building for?

One short phrase about their life context.
02 Primary Goal

What does success look like?

In one or two sentences. This prints directly onto their plan cover and shapes every page after it.

03 FRA Priorities

Your top mobility priorities.

Top 3–5 from the screen, in IMS format: "Joint Direction Side". Examples: Hip IR L+R · Left Shoulder ER · Ankle Plantarflexion + Eversion · Thoracic Extension · Bilateral Hamstring.

04 Mobility Map

Every joint, rated.

Traffic-light rating for every joint you screened. Add rows as you go.

05 Strength Markers

What did you test today?

Add every strength exercise you actually tested, with the result. Example results · "18 reps (30s)" · "140 lbs × 3" · "45 sec hold" · "6 × 40 lbs".

06 Constraints

What do we work around?

Check any that apply. These change which exercises the generator picks.

07 Body Composition (optional)

What the numbers say.

If BOD POD was done this visit. Leave blank otherwise. RMR, TDEE, and nutrition targets auto-calculate from lean mass using Katch-McArdle.

08 Nutrition Strategy

Fuel for the work.

Pick one strategy and one activity factor. Drives the macro split on the plan.

09 Coach Notes

What did you notice?

Anything else worth capturing · how they moved, what they said, pain patterns, what they're currently doing, what they've tried.
